Genie.

While writing "Omega Chess Genie" I met with errors in compiler, so I decided to fix them. My contrubutions to Genie:
  • Comments at the end of blocks break compilation: https://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=611085 (link in new tab)
  • Add possibility to write enumeration values into one line separated by comma: https://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=776833 (link in new tab)

  • Added ability to define methods in enums: https://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=776835 (link in new tab)
